Dosage

Acetylsalicylic acid is prescribed orally after meals in tablet form. Usual doses for adults as an analgesic and antipyretic (for febrile illnesses, headaches, migraines, neuralgia) are 0.25-0.5-1 g 3-4 times a day; for children, depending on age, from 0.1 to 0.3 g per dose.
